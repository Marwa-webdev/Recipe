Margherita Pizza

Classic pizza with fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough base
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 100g mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Spread tomato sauce on dough.
  3. Top with mozzarella and basil leaves.
  4. Bake 10–12 minutes until cheese melts and crust is golden.
  5. Drizzle olive oil, slice, and serve.
