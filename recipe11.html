Momos

Delicious steamed dumplings stuffed with vegetables or meat, served with spicy dipping sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables or minced meat
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spicy dipping sauce

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour and water to make a soft dough, rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Prepare filling with vegetables or meat, season well.
  3. Roll dough into small circles, place filling, and fold into momos.
  4. Steam for 10â€“12 minutes until cooked.
  5. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
