Fluffy Pancakes

Light and fluffy pancakes served with maple syrup and fresh fruits.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Whisk in milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly greased skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter to form pancakes, cook until golden on both sides.
  5. Serve warm with syrup and fruits.
