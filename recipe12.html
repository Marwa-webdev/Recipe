Chowmein

Stir-fried noodles with vegetables and your choice of protein, tossed in a savory sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 200g noodles
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 100g chicken or tofu (optional)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp chili sauce (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions and drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, sauté garlic and vegetables (and chicken if using).
  3. Add noodles, soy sauce, and chili sauce, stir-fry for 3–5 minutes.
  4. Mix well, garnish with green onions, and serve hot.
