Caesar Salad

Crisp romaine lettuce tossed with creamy Caesar dressing, croutons, and shaved parmesan.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and chop lettuce.
  2. Toss with dressing, croutons, and parmesan.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, serve chilled.
