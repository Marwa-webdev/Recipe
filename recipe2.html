Chocolate Cake

Rich and moist chocolate cake topped with silky chocolate ganache.

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a cake pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add milk, eggs, and oil; beat until smooth.
  4. Pour into pan, bake 30–35 minutes.
  5. Cool and frost with chocolate ganache.
