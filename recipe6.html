Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish with creamy sauce, pancetta, and parmesan cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta with garlic until crisp.
  3. Beat eggs and parmesan together.
  4. Combine spaghetti with pancetta and remove from heat.
  5. Quickly mix in egg mixture, season, and serve.
