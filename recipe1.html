Strawberry Custard

A creamy custard layered with fresh strawberries, perfect for a sweet dessert or afternoon treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Fresh strawberries for topping

Instructions:

  1. Heat the milk until warm but not boiling.
  2. Whisk egg yolks and sugar together in a bowl.
  3. Slowly pour hot milk into the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
  4. Return to pan and cook on low heat until thickened.
  5. Pour into serving dishes, chill, and top with fresh strawberries.
