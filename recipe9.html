Chocolate Brownies

Decadent chocolate brownies with a fudgy center and crisp edges.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a pan.
  2. Melt butter and mix with sugar.
  3. Add eggs and cocoa powder, mix well.
  4. Stir in flour, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Bake 20–25 minutes, cool, slice, and serve.
