Salmon Teriyaki

Grilled salmon glazed with a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce, served with steamed vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1 garlic clove, minced

Instructions:

  1. Mix soy sauce, honey, vinegar, ginger, and garlic for sauce.
  2. Marinate salmon for 30 minutes.
  3. Grill salmon on medium heat for 5â€“7 minutes per side.
  4. Brush with remaining sauce, serve with vegetables.
