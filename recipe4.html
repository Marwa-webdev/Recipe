Fresh Fruit Salad

A colorful mix of seasonal fruits drizzled with a light honey-lime dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups diced strawberries
  • 2 cups diced mango
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup grapes
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lime juice

Instructions:

  1. Combine all fruits in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk honey and lime juice, pour over fruits.
  3. Toss gently to coat evenly.
  4. Chill for 30 minutes and serve.
